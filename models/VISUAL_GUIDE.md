## ğŸ¯ Recommended Printing Order

For a complete dev workspace setup:

1. **Start Small:** Project Tag Holder (1 hour)
   - Quick print to test settings
   - Immediate utility for labeling equipment

2. **Desk Organization:** Desk Nameplate (2 hours)
   - Professional appearance
   - Good for team identity

3. **Cable Management:** Cable Organizer (3-4 hours)
   - High impact on desk cleanliness
   - Daily quality-of-life improvement

4. **Storage Solution:** Small Parts Organizer (2-3 hours)
   - Keeps desk drawer organized
   - Prevents lost adapters and drives

5. **Ergonomics:** Laptop Stand (8-12 hours)
   - Largest time investment
   - Significant health and productivity benefits
   - Print overnight or over weekend
